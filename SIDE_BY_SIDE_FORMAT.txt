╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                         HOW TO INTERPRET                                     ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

For Each Lead:

1. Look at LEFT (Original - Blue)
   • This is the ground truth from CSV
   • What the signal should look like

2. Look at RIGHT (Predicted - Red)
   • This is what the model extracted from image
   • What the model thinks the signal is

3. Compare Left vs Right
   ✅ Similar shapes = Good prediction
   ✅ Same peaks/valleys = Excellent
   ❌ Different patterns = Poor prediction
   ❌ Shifted or scaled = Needs improvement

4. Check Metrics
   • SNR > 20 dB = Excellent match
   • RMSE < 0.1 = Very good accuracy
