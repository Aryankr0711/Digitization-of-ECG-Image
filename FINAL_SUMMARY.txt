╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

How to Read the Plots:

1. LOOK LEFT (Original - Blue)
   • Ground truth from CSV file
   • What the signal actually is
   • Reference for comparison

2. LOOK RIGHT (Predicted - Red)
   • Model's prediction from image
   • What the model extracted
   • Check against original

3. COMPARE
   ✅ Similar waveforms = Good
   ✅ Same peaks/valleys = Excellent
   ✅ High SNR (>20 dB) = Very accurate
   ❌ Different shapes = Poor
   ❌ Low SNR (<15 dB) = Needs work

4. CHECK METRICS
   • SNR box on right side
   • Quick quality indicator
   • Per-lead performance
